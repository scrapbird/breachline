# BreachLine Pricing

BreachLine consists of a free application with paid premium features.

After purchasing a license, users can use the Workspace feature, which allows them to organize and annotate data files.

In addition, users can purhcase a subscription to the workspace sync service, which allows them to automatically sync their workspace configurations and annotations to the cloud and share them with their team members to work colaboratively on an investigation.

## Premium Pricing

The premium license can be purchased for 10 USD per month, or for 100 USD for a yearly license.

## Workspace Sync Service

The workspace sync service pricing is set at 5 USD for 5 online workspaces. Additional workspaces can be purchased 5 at a time for 5 USD per month. (5 USD per month for 5 workspaces, 10 USD per month for 10 workspaces)

To share these workspaces with team members, additional seats can be purchased for 1 USD per seat. Adding 1 user to 1 workspace uses up 1 seat.

If you have the base 5 online workspaces and you wish to add a team member to two of them, you will need to purchase 2 additional seats for 2 USD per month.

Users who are invited to an online workspace do not have to own a subscription for workspace sync service, but they do require a valid premium license. All sync costs are paid by the owner of the workspace.
