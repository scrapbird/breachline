### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What file formats are supported?**  
A: BreachLine supports CSV, XLSX (Excel), and JSON files. For JSON files, you can specify custom JPATH expressions to locate the data.

**Q: Can I upgrade from Free to Premium?**  
A: Yes! Simply purchase a Premium license and enter the license key in the application.

**Q: What's the difference in file size limits?**  
A: The Free version supports files up to 1 GB, while Premium handles files up to 5-10 GB efficiently using advanced caching and external sorting.

**Q: What are the subscription options?**  
A: Premium is available as either a monthly subscription ($10/month) or yearly subscription ($60/year). You can choose to auto-renew or let it expire at the end of the period.

**Q: Can I cancel my subscription?**  
A: Yes! You can disable auto-renewal at any time. Your license will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
